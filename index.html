My Dearest Lorence,
As I sit here and think of you, my heart swells with so much love that words barely seem enough to express how I feel.
But still, I find myself compelled to try, hoping that in some way,
these words will reach you the way my heart does every time I think of you.
From the moment we met, something shifted in me.
I didn’t realize it then, but now I know—my life was forever changed.
You brought a light into my world that I never knew was missing, and in your presence, everything feels right.
It’s in the way you smile, the warmth of your voice, and the kindness you show the world.
You have a way of making the ordinary seem extraordinary, and my days are brighter simply because you’re in them.
I cherish every little moment we share, every laugh, every conversation, and every quiet moment spent in each other’s company.
You’ve become my best friend, my confidant, and my greatest love.
There is no one else I would rather spend my time with than you.
I want you to know that I am here for you, in every moment, through every challenge, and in all the joys that life brings.
My love for you is endless, a constant in a world that changes so quickly.
I am thankful for you every single day, and I cannot wait to continue writing our story together.
With all my heart,
-Sadness
